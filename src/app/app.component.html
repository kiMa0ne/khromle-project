Welcome to Khromle

Khromle is a TAROKKATA of Chromle, a Wordl inspired game of colours! The objective is to guess the RGB code of the colour displayed in the central panel. Every day you will be given a new colour to guess. Today's colour is

With each guess you will receive hints guiding you towards the answer in the form of colours under the groups (R, G and B) or the digits of your guess. GREEN groups are quite correct, and YELLOW groups are very, very close. ORANGE ones mean they are practically the same number as the target group, while RED groups are a little off but still in reach. PURPLE identifies digits that would have been or are correct in a different position but is only shown when no other hints are present for the digit or group in that position. WHITE digits are entirely incorrect.
